Nepal is a beautiful country located in South Asia, nestled between China and India.
It is renowned for its stunning Himalayan mountain range, including Mount Everest.
Kathmandu is the capital city and is known for its rich history and vibrant culture.
Nepal is home to diverse ethnic groups and languages, creating a unique cultural tapestry.
The country is famous for its ancient temples, monasteries, and UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
Tourism is a major industry, attracting trekkers, climbers, and adventure seekers from around the world.
Nepal has a rich tradition of festivals, music, and dance celebrated throughout the year.
The natural beauty of Nepal includes lush forests, rivers, and wildlife reserves.
Nepalese cuisine features delicious dishes like momo, dal bhat, and sel roti.
Despite its small size, Nepal has made significant contributions to art, literature, and spirituality.
